About Ubisoft

Ubisoft is a leading creator, publisher, and distributor of interactive entertainment and services. Founded in 1986, Ubisoft has grown to become one of the most innovative companies in the gaming industry, known for delivering unforgettable gaming experiences to players around the world.

Our Mission

At Ubisoft, we strive to enrich players' lives with original and memorable gaming experiences. We believe in the power of play to bring people together, inspire creativity, and spark joy across the globe.

Our Journey

  • 1986: Ubisoft was founded by the Guillemot brothers in France.
  • 1989: First game developed and released: "Zombi" for the Amstrad CPC.
  • 1995: Creation of the iconic "Rayman" franchise.
  • 2007: Launch of the "Assassin's Creed" franchise, one of the most popular series in gaming history.
  • 2015: Ubisoft pioneers open-world multiplayer games with "The Division" and "Rainbow Six Siege."
  • 2023: Focuses on expanding into new platforms, including cloud gaming and VR.

Our Values

Ubisoft is built on the pillars of creativity, collaboration, and innovation. We aim to empower our teams to take bold steps, challenge the status quo, and continue delivering high-quality experiences to gamers everywhere.
